About Us

Dream Doggy Daycare where your dog's happiness, safety, and well-being are at the heart of everything we do. We are a dedicated team of pet care experts with years of experience in providing exceptional services tailored to every dog's unique needs. From cozy overnight stays to extended vacation care, our facility offers a safe, fun, and engaging environment where your dog can truly feel at home. We believe that every dog deserves personalized attention and care, which is why we offer a range of services beyond boarding, including grooming, training, and customized activity plans.

Our skilled groomers ensure that your dog looks and feels their best, while our experienced trainers help reinforce positive behaviors and skills. At Dream Doggy Daycare, we’re more than just a place for your dog to stay—we’re a place where they can thrive. When your dog is with us, they become part of our family, receiving the same level of love, attention, and care they would at home. We look forward to welcoming you and your furry friend to Dream Doggy Daycare and making every stay memorable, safe, and tail-wagging fun.

  • Experienced and Certified Staff: Trained in animal behavior, first aid, and positive reinforcement techniques.
  • Safe and Secure Facility: Fully fenced play areas, secure entry systems, and constant supervision for peace of mind.
  • Large Outdoor and Indoor Play Areas: Spacious, clean, and climate-controlled areas to accommodate dogs of all sizes and energy levels.
  • Separate Play Zones: Designated areas for small, medium, and large dogs to ensure safe play.
  • Socialization Opportunities: Group play sessions designed to help dogs build social skills in a structured environment.
  • Splash Pad and Doggy Water Park: Water play areas for cooling off and fun in a safe, supervised environment.
  • Personalized Care Plans: Tailored care based on each dog’s temperament, activity level, and any special requirements.
  • Nap Time and Quiet Areas: Cozy spaces for rest and relaxation between play sessions.
